Pasta recipe

Pasta: A tasty and easy recipe

This recipe is simple and easy to make which you should try at your home You can follow the ingredients and making guidelines here:

ingredients

  • Penne pasta: 200g pasta
  • Spices: chilli, balck pepper, salt
  • Fresh Produce: ginger, garlic, curry leaves
  • milk: 2 cups
  • Oil: 1/4 cup
  • cheeses and butter: Parmesan, mozzarella, and butter
  • chicken: 1/2 cup small cube chicken

How to Make Pasta Step-By-Step

  1. boil the Pasta in hot water and keep aside
  2. melt the butter, add the flour cook 2 minutes.
  3. Gradually pour in the milk. This should take about 5-7 minutes.
  4. the sauce has thickened, add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce.
  6. Mix well to coat the pasta evenly and cook together.
