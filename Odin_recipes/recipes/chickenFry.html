Chicken Fry

Chicken Fry: A tasty and easy recipe

This recipe is simple and easy to make which you should try at your home You can follow the ingredients and making guidelines here:

ingredients

  • Chicken: boneless or bonny
  • Fresh Produce: ginger, garlic, curry leaves
  • Spices: chilli, coriander, turmeric and garam masala
  • Lemon/vinegar: 1 table spoon
  • Oil: 1/2 cup
  • egg: 1 piece

How to Make chicken fry Step-By-Step

  1. Take garlic, ginger and curry leaves in a blender.
  2. Take chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, pepper powder, garam masala powder, turmeric powder and salt in a bowl
  3. Add lemon juice or vinegar
  4. Add all purpose flour and mix well
  5. Add water and make paste
  6. Add chicken cubes, mix and marinate for 2h
  7. deep fry till golden brown color appears
